About Us

Welcome to The Sisters Quran Club

📖
Who We Are
A group of sisters coming together to talk about the wonderful Book of Allah ✨ and various topics around it such as the sunnah, hadith and helpful duas.
🌸
What Makes Us Special
This is a club where anyone is free to share their opinions, inspirational quotes and ask questions. It is interactive and welcoming to all sisters 🙌
🤝
Team Challenges
We believe we can achieve more by working together. From Ramadan challenges to Trivia Tuesdays — teamwork makes the dream work! 🌸
📅
Monthly Topics
Each month we cover topics voted by members — from Tafsir to Tajweed to holy months on the Hijri calendar. We teach each other new things!
