"Indeed, Allah has ninety-nine names — one hundred minus one. Whoever preserves them (ahsaha — knows, understands, and acts upon them) will enter Paradise."

Sahih al-Bukhari 2736 & Sahih Muslim 2677 Narrated by Abu Hurayrah (رضي الله عنه)

Scholars explain that ahsaha encompasses three levels: memorising the names, understanding their meanings, and supplicating to Allah through them — exactly what this tool is designed to help you do.
